Peanut Butter Milkshake Recipe

Nothing goes together quite like ice cream and peanut butter! For all the peanut butter lovers out there, here's a peanut butter milkshake to satify those cravings.

Ingredients

  • Vanilla ice cream (or the ice cream of your choice).
  • Peanut butter
  • Milk
  • Ice
  • Whipped cream

Recipe

  1. Blend milk, ice cream, and ice together until consistency is smooth.
  2. Enjoy!
