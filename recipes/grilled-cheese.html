Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Craving something savory? A classic grilled cheese sandwich is sure to hit the spot no matter your age! Use melty cheddar cheese and plenty of butter in this unhealthy recipe.

Ingredients

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Bread
  • Butter

Recipe

  1. Spread butter evenly across one side of bread.
  2. Put bread butter side down on skillet. Turn burner to medium high heat. Add cheese and then top with second slice of bread. Butter the top slice of bread.
  3. Let cook until golden brown. Flip and repeat on other side.
  4. Make sure cheese is fully melted and enjoy.
